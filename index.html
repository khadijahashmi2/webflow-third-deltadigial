We bagged the 2025 Innovation Award!

Kudos to everyone! Your hard work and dedication helped us win Best Innovator in the Spring Business Awards last Sunday. We bested twelve other nominees in the category, thanks to our diverse portfolio and tech-driven approach.

Our president and CEO Gustav Shaffer expressed his gratitude to the team, saying "This year, we really outdid ourselves. We produced out-of-the-box solutions that truly made an impact - and we can do so much more. This is just the beginning.

"This award is the latest in a string of company-wide achievements. Last year, Dempsey and Delta bagged the Best Employer and Best Agency Awards from the Fortes Institute. This is our first win from the Spring Business Awards.
